Why we exist

India doesn't have a sports infrastructure problem — it has a connection problem. The fans, the players, the venues, and the stories are all there. What's missing is the layer that ties it together: one app that lets you follow every sport you care about, book the court down the road, find players at your level, and watch the stories mainstream media ignores.

That's what Outdoor is. Not another social app. Not a booking widget. The complete sports ecosystem India forgot to build.
